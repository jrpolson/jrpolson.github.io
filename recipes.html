Breakfast Recipes

1. Best Breakfast Wrap Ever - Inspired by the Starbucks Spinach Eggwhite + Feta Wrap (it's even better!)

Ingredients
• Lavash bread
• Whipped cream cheese
• Feta
• 1/4 avocado
• 2 eggs or egg whites
• Spinach
• Sun dried tomato
• TJs chili crunch oil
• Honey
Process
1. Spread a thin layer of the cream cheese on lavash bread.
2. Sprinkle feta all over cream cheese.
3. Add sun dried tomatoes over feta, spaced out evenly.
4. Slice avocado and spread out on top.
5. Scramble eggs with spinach until fully wilted and cooked; add salt and pepper.
6. Put scrambled egg mixture on top of lavash bread.
7. Top with chili crunch oil and a drizzle of honey.
8. Roll like a burrito.
9. Pan sear each side for about a minute until crispy and golden brown.
10. Slice in half, and BAM!

2. Sweet Potato Pesto Bowl

Ingredients
• Sweet potato
• Spinach
• 1/4 avocado
• Red onion
• Feta
• 2 eggs
• TJs pesto
• Cottage cheese (optional)
• Balsamic glaze
• TJs chili crunch oil
• Optional sprinkle of cilantro
Process
1. Dice sweet potato and season with salt, pepper, garlic, paprika, and olive oil. Air fry at 400°F for about 10 minutes.
2. Dice red onion and avocado, and place in a bowl along with spinach.
3. Cook eggs in pesto and cottage cheese (optional for extra protein). Cover and cook on medium until eggs are just slightly jiggly.
4. Add finished sweet potato to the bowl.
5. Sprinkle feta.
6. Add eggs, and top with chili crunch, balsamic glaze, and cilantro.

Lunch and Dinner Recipes

1. Salmon Tacos with Avocado Cottage Crema and Mango Pico

Ingredients
• 2 carb savvy smaller tortillas from TJs (or any smaller tortilla)
• Salmon
• 1/4 avocado
• About 1/4–1/3 cup cottage cheese
• Lime juice
• Cilantro
• Mango pico salsa from TJs (GAS)
• Feta
• Seasonings: honey, olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, chili powder, cumin, garlic
Process
1. Put your avocado, cottage cheese, lime juice, cilantro, salt, pepper, garlic, and about a quarter cup of water in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth, and add more water if needed.
3. Set your air fryer to 400°, and cut your salmon in cubes and add it to a bowl. Add a drizzle of olive oil, honey, and the rest of the seasonings and mix.
4. Air fry for about 5–7 minutes.
5. In the meantime, put a generous amount of the crema on both tortillas.
6. Then top with salmon, mango pico salsa, feta, and a sprinkle of cilantro.

2. Greek Turkey Meatballs with Potatoes (or you can use rice!) and Salad

Ingredients
Meatballs:
• Ground turkey
• Dill
• Dijon mustard (adds so much flavor and moisture)
• Breadcrumbs
• Olive oil
• Lemon juice
• Seasonings: salt, pepper, oregano, garlic powder, onion powder
Potatoes:
• Fingerling potatoes
• Olive oil
• Paprika, salt, pepper, garlic, lemon juice
Greek Salad:
• English cucumber
• Cherry tomato
• Red onion
• Feta
• Olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, garlic, oregano
*Also added hummus on top*
Process
1. Halve potatoes and mix them with olive oil and seasonings. Air fry at 400°F until crispy and golden (about 5–10 minutes, flipping halfway).
2. Mix ground turkey with all meatball ingredients (eyeball the measurements — about 1 tsp Dijon and a small handful of breadcrumbs).
3. Roll into balls (about 2 tbsp each) and cook on a pan over medium heat, covered. Flip once golden brown on the bottom (cook about 5–7 minutes total).
4. For the salad, chop all veggies and add feta, lemon juice, olive oil, and seasonings.
5. Add hummus on top and serve!
