Restaurant Favorites

Top 5 Madison Restaurant Favorites

If you've been to Madison before, you know how many amazing restaurant options there are here — and it can be overwhelming! Here are my top 5 eats in Madison (in no particular order):

  1. Estacion Inka

    Estacion Inka is a Peruvian restaurant with a casual vibe, spectacular food, and large portions. My favorite thing to order is the chicken saltado with cilantro rice — it never disappoints.

  2. Eno Vino

    Eno Vino is a more upscale restaurant with a stunning view of the Capitol and an amazing atmosphere. They're known for their tapas (small plates to share). My favorites include the chicken kebabs, tortellini, scallops, and cauliflower — their flavor combinations are truly unmatched!

  3. Marigold

    Marigold is my favorite breakfast/brunch spot in Madison. The atmosphere is cozy and charming (expect a line on weekends!). I love the mushroom and gouda omelet or the breakfast burrito — and the breakfast potatoes are incredible. Honorable mention: the coffee is phenomenal!

  4. Sardine

    I haven’t been to Sardine too many times, but every visit has been great. It’s right on the water and beautifully designed inside — plus, it’s one of the only French restaurants in Madison. Everything is fresh, simple, and delicious.

  5. Lucille

    Lucille is another charming restaurant near the Capitol. It serves creative pizzas and a mix of fusion dishes. My go-to is the Umami Pie — definitely my favorite. And their cheese curds? Amazing.
